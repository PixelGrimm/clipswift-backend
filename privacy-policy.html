ClipSwift Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 23, 2025

ClipSwift is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Data Collection

ClipSwift collects the following types of data:

1. Snippets and User Content

  • Text Snippets: Your saved text snippets are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
  • Commands: Custom commands you create for your snippets (e.g., ":hello")
  • Categories: How you organize your snippets
  • Settings: Theme preferences and user tier information

2. Payment Information

  • Payment Processing: All payments are processed securely through Stripe
  • No Card Storage: We never store your credit card information
  • Subscription Data: Basic subscription status and billing information

3. AI Content Generation

  • Prompts: Text prompts you send for AI content generation
  • Generated Content: AI-generated responses for your snippets
  • Processing: All AI requests are processed through OpenAI's API

4. Usage Analytics

  • Auto-completion: Basic usage data for snippet auto-completion functionality
  • Feature Usage: Which features you use most frequently
  • Performance: Basic performance metrics to improve the extension

Data Usage

We use your data only for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To provide snippet auto-completion across websites
  • Payment Processing: To process payments for premium features
  • AI Generation: To generate content based on your prompts
  • Improvement: To improve the extension's functionality and user experience

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Snippets and settings are stored locally in your browser
  • Secure Servers: Payment and AI processing data on secure servers
  • No Personal Data: We don't store personal information on our servers

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data with trusted third-party services:

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing and subscription management
  • OpenAI: For AI content generation based on your prompts
  • Railway: For hosting our backend services

Legal Requirements

  • We may share data if required by law or to protect our rights
  • We will notify you of any legal requests for your data

Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Secure APIs: All third-party integrations use secure APIs
  • Local Storage: Your snippets are stored securely in your browser
  • No Access: We cannot access your locally stored snippets

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your stored snippets in the extension
  • Delete: You can delete snippets and data at any time
  • Export: You can export your snippets for backup
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the extension removes all local data

Children's Privacy

ClipSwift is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
